Batman

Bruce Wayne
Powers: exceptional martial artist, combat strategy, inexhaustible wealth, brilliant deductive skill, advanced technology

Bruce Wayne is a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and industrialist who resides in Gotham City. He swears vengeance against criminals after witnessing the murder of his parents Thomas and Martha as a child. He trains himself physically and intellectually, crafts a bat-inspired persona, and monitors the Gotham streets at night. He has sidekicks such as Robin and Batgirl and helpers such as Alfred Pennyworth, James Gordon, and Catwoman. His archenemy is the Joker and other enemies such as the Penguin, the Riddler, and Two-Face.

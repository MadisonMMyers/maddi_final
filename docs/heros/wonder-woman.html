Wonder Woman

Diana Prince
Powers: super strength, invulnerability, flight, combat skill, combat strategy, superhuman agility, healing factor, magic weaponry

Diana is from the island nation of Themyscira. Her official title is Princess Diana of Themyscira. When blending into the society outside of her homeland, she sometimes adopts her civilian identity, Diana Prince. She is the biological daughter of Zeus and Hippolyta, jointly raised by her mother and her aunts Antiope and Menalippe. Her enemies bent on destroying her include Cheetah, Ares, Circe, Doctor Poison, Giganta, Blue Snowman, and Doctor Cyber. She is also a part of the superhero teams Justice Society and Justice League.

The Teen Titans

TEAM: Nightwing, Starfire, Cyborg, Raven, Beastboy
FIRST APPEARANCE: The Brave and The Bold #54 (1964)

The Teen Titans are an organization of young vigilantes banded together to fight crime. They began as a group of sidekicks looking to distinguish themselves from their mentors, they would go on to expand into a worldwide establishment.

Catwoman

Selina Kyle
Powers: exceptional martial artist, gymnastic ability, combat skill, master thief and cat burglar, customized weapons

Selina was raised alongside her sister Maggie by a drunken father who habitually mistreated her mother. they later committed suicide when Selina was young. During her childhood, she stole costumes and a gun, which she used to threaten a bunch of bullies who were threatening a boy. She later became a Gotham City expert thief who crossed paths with Batman on many occasions. Having largely left behind her villainous ways, she now operates as a frequent ally of the Batman Family.

Deathstroke

Slade Wilson
Powers: exceptional martial artist, combat strategy, durability, enhanced strength and speed, weapons expert, superhuman agility, master swordsman

Salde is the world's best and most expensive mercenary. When he was 16 he lied about his age and joined the army. He met his wife Adeline Kane who trained him to be the best fighter. Wilson volunteered for a medical experiment designed to stimulate his adrenal gland in the hopes of increasing a soldier's ability. The experiment did not go as expected, and Slade fell into a coma. When he awakened, he discovered that he was now capable of using 90% of his brain capacity, and his strength, durability, and agility were increased to near-superhuman levels. After mercenaries kill one of his sons, leading to his wife leaving him, he starts to use his abilities as a mercenary himself. He is a known enemy of The Teen Titans and Beast Boy.

Black Manta

David Hyde
Powers: enhanced strength, energy blasts, skilled fighter, unrivaled swimmer, master swordsman, advanced technology, intelligence

David Hyde is a descendant of the lost tribe of Atlanteans known as the Deserters. His mother Ramona walked out on the family when he was a child and he was raised by his father Jesse on the family fishing boat, the Black Manta. After an altercation that ended with both, Author and David's fathers dying, David has wanted revenge ever since. He has been on many supervillains teams such as the Legion of Doom the Injustice League and the Secret Society of Super-Villains.
